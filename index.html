Step-by-Step Guidance

Clear, actionable steps for navigating arrest situations

1

Remain Silent & Request a Lawyer

Exercise your Fifth Amendment right. Say "I want to speak to a lawyer" and nothing else until legal representation arrives.

2

Contact Someone You Trust

You have the right to one phone call. Use it to contact a trusted family member or friend who can help arrange legal representation.

3

Understand Your Charges

Listen carefully when charges are read. Don't admit guilt or discuss details. Your lawyer will help you understand the legal implications.

4

Prepare for Bail Hearing

Work with your attorney to present factors that favor release: community ties, employment, and lack of flight risk.

Supporting Your Loved One

Guidance for family and friends during legal crises

Immediate Steps to Take

  • Stay calm and gather important information about the arrest
  • Contact a criminal defense attorney immediately
  • Gather funds for potential bail or legal fees
  • Document everything and keep detailed records

Communication Guidelines

  • All jail calls are recorded - speak carefully
  • Don't discuss case details over the phone
  • Focus on emotional support and practical needs
  • Keep conversations positive and encouraging
